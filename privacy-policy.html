CritBot - Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This policy explains how CritBot collects and stores information.

  • Data collected: Discord user ID, username, and in-game progress (levels, Miscrits, etc.).
  • Purpose: To save and manage gameplay data for each user.
  • Storage: Data is stored securely in a private database, not shared with third parties.
  • Deletion: Users may request data deletion through the support Discord server.
  • Third-party services: CritBot does not use cookies, ads, or tracking analytics.

Contact: via the official CritBot Discord support server.
